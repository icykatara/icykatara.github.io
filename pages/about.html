Jeanne Chan is a ceramicist and software engineer. She started ceramics at 17 years old while taking a course at Normandale Community College with Lazare Rottach.

She stopped ceramics to pursue her Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with a minor in Neuroscience. A reality check, but also another passion of hers. Finishing her degree at 19 years of age, she moved to O’ahu, Hawai’i to live her love story.

She started pottery again at Hawai’i Potters’ Guild, studying under fantastic and wildly talented artists: Esther Nowell, Joy Sanchez, and Harinani Orme. All in support of the beginning Jeanne’s handbuilt ceramic pieces.

She is now back to pursuing a Master’s in Computer Science at the University of Hawai’i at Manoa while continuing her ceramic masterpieces.
